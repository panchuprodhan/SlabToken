# Usage:

-> To add tokens to the contract, use the addToken function, specifying the amount of token in the respective field.
-> To view the balance of the contract, use the "balance" function.
-> To view how many tokens are stored in the slab, click on the "slab4", which goes on as a descending order.
-> FOr viewing the current slab, click on the "viewSlab" functionality.

# Features:

-> Balance, to check the total amount of tokens in the contract.
-> Slab4, cap of 500 * 10 ** 18 tokens.
-> Slab3, cap of 400 * 10 ** 18 tokens.
-> Slab2, cap of 300 * 10 ** 18 tokens.
-> Slab1, cap of 200 * 10 ** 18 tokens.
-> Slab0, cap of 100 * 10 ** 18 tokens.
-> ViewSlab, to view the active slab.
